ABOUT

Certified NASM Trainer dedicated to making a positive change in other people’s lives. I am a firm believer that everyone has the ability to change and reach their personal goals with proper guidance and enough motivation for attendance. In the past, I have made some positive changes in the lives of others. I have helped people maintain a healthy lifestyle, follow a proper diet plan, lose weight, increase their overall strength, correct postural deficiencies and radiate a high level of confidence. The leading causes of discontinuing a workout plan is a result of an injury incorrect form during exercise. I emphasize proper exercise technique and safety in order to prevent potential injuries.

EXPERTISE

Specialty training in Boxing, Muay Thai, Body Building and Full Body Conditioning in order to increase overall Strength-Endurance as well as Cardio-Endurance.

PHILOSOPHY

It’s not always what you know as a trainer and what type of workout you can provide. What matters the most is a trainer’s ability to adapt to others needs, in order to provide the best possible strategy for one to meet their personal goals.
